Introduction

Our Company is a new venture that has been created for the Indian audience. Our focus is to provide customized wear, apparel and accessories and position ourselves as the top fashion venture servicing this market. Our intentions are to obtain market shares and become a central hub of shopping activity for all Indians as well as outsiders, who enjoy wearing fashionable apparel. We believe that this is critical to our initial success and long-term growth.


Our Business is focused for the quality and style conscious customers, providing unique, eclectic and affordable products. We intend to win customers through the wide range of products that we have & the unique services that we are offering. We encourage the creativity and promotion of individualism through style and fashion at affordable prices.
• To provide people with a platform that offers a comfortable and approachable environment.
• Focus on Quality, Design and Price.
• Wide range of products able to cater in female fashion segments.
• To receive a 40% profit margin with in the first year.
• To have a customer base of 1,000 by the end of the first operating year.
• To create employment opportunity.
• To showcase quality, well-constructed fashions from prominent and cutting-edge designers.
• To offer a variety of beautiful and high-end fashion accessories.
• To help people learn what clothing and styles go best with their unique personalities.
• To conserve and uphold culture and values by providing domestic products.
